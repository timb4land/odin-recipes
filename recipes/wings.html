Chicken wings

These wings will melt in your mouth and you will be craving for more

Ingredients

  • 600g of Hot chicken wings
  • 30-50g of Mayonnaise
  • Garlic powder
  • Mix of dry herbs including at least parsley and chives

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 C
  2. Make the mayonnaise by mixing mayonnaise and the spices
  3. Put the wings inside the oven for 25 minutes
  4. Take them off the oven, let them rest for 10 minutes
  5. Dip the wings into the mayonnaise and eat.
